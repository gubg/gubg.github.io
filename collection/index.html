Collection

This is a list of all the board games we know of at Georgetown University. The games that the library owns have been donated by our club and are available for all students at checkout. Most of them are games that have been donated to the club by companies. The games owned by the SAC can be checked out by a Georgetown University club. Games on the wishlist are games that our club plans to buy in the future with our SAC funding.
